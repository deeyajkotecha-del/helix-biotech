Atopic Dermatitis: Intensely Pruritic, Chronic and Underserved Disease
Opportunity Exists to Transform the Treatment Paradigm

   • Chronic inflammatory disease that causes inflamed and irritated skin,
     making it itchy and painful

   • Majority of patients have disease onset as children and carry the disease
     burden into adulthood

   • Can significantly impact a patient’s quality of life, disrupting daily activities

   • Common comorbidities include asthma and allergic rhinitis

   • Many options only address symptoms, not underlying Type 2 inflammation

   • Estimated 43 million adults in US/EU5/JP1

   • Less than 1 million patients currently on dupilumab2
