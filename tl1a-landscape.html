Investment Thesis

Bull Case

  • 🟢 First new biologic MOA for IBD in 5+ years; validated by 3 independent Ph2 successes
  • 🟢 Dual anti-inflammatory + anti-fibrotic — may alter CD disease progression (no other drug does this)
  • 🟢 Peak sales $4-5B tulisokibart alone; class could reach $15-20B across indications by 2034
  • 🟢 Expanding beyond IBD: SSc-ILD, atopic dermatitis, HS, RA, axSpA — massive optionality
  • 🟢 Genetic biomarker CDx (Merck) enables precision medicine — higher response rates
  • 🟢 Favorable safety across all Ph2 programs; potential for combos with IL-23i / integrin
  • 🟢 Next-gen (SPY002, XmAb942) offer Q12W-Q6M dosing — best-in-class convenience

Bear Case

  • 🔴 Crowded: 45+ programs, 3 Ph3 — pricing erosion as competitors launch within ~2 years
  • 🔴 No Phase 3 data yet; ATLAS-UC Nov 2026 — high binary risk ahead
  • 🔴 Cross-trial comparisons unreliable: different populations, endpoints, placebo rates
  • 🔴 Fibrosis benefit unproven in humans — preclinical promise may not translate
  • 🔴 Existing SOC strong: Skyrizi ($12B+), Rinvoq ($8B+), Entyvio ($5B+) entrenched
  • 🔴 Biosimilar Humira flood — payer pressure on all branded IBD biologics
  • 🔴 Long-term safety unknown; DcR3 biology complex — potential off-target effects

Patent & IP Landscape

ESTIMATED EXCLUSIVITY WINDOWS

Biologics: 12yr data exclusivity (US) from BLA approval. Directional estimates.

KEY IP NOTES

Tulisokibart (Merck)

Prometheus CoM patents ~2019-2020. CDx biomarker patent extends commercial moat. Key: US10689439B2. Approved ~2028 → biosimilar ~2040.

Afimkibart (Roche)

Originally Pfizer (PF-06480605), CoM ~2016-2017 (earliest in class). Pfizer retains ex-US/Japan. Approved ~2028 → biosimilar ~2040.

Duvakitug (Sanofi/Teva)

Unique IgG1-λ2, selective DR3 binding. CoM ~2020-2021. $500M Ph3 milestone paid Q4 2025.

Next-Gen (SPY002, XmAb942)

Half-life extension tech patents (Xencor Xtend, Spyre). ~75d t½ vs ~20d gen-1. Engineering patents create barriers post-CoM expiry.
